The Galactic Odyssey

In the year 3099, humanity faces extinction as mysterious invaders from a distant galaxy approach Earth. Captain Aurora, a fearless space explorer, must lead a daring mission to save the planet. With time running out and enemies on all sides, she discovers that the key to survival lies not only in technology, but in the strength of the human spirit.
